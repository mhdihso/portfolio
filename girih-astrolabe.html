Girih & Astrolabe

generative building blocks · turquoise, gold, terracotta

1 · Girih tiling — page background generator

A hexagonal lattice of 10‑point star knots and connecting straps, drawn as SVG and animated in like a build compiling, tile by tile.

2 · Astrolabe — loading spinner

Concentric rings + graduated limb + rotating rete, in place of a generic spinner.
Three independent layers: the limb (outer ring, fixed, tick‑marked in 5° steps), the mater (middle ring, static), and the rete (star pattern, rotates continuously) — mirroring how a real astrolabe's star‑map plate turns above its fixed base. Drop this in as a favicon‑scale mark or a full loading state.
Both pieces are plain SVG + CSS + a small JS generator — no image assets, no libraries. Copy the two <script> blocks directly into a site.
