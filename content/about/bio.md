I am **Mehdi Hosseini**, a Backend & Distributed Systems Engineer and Computer Science Master's student at the **University of Tübingen**. I have **5+ years of hands-on engineering experience** spanning backend development, distributed systems design, DevOps infrastructure, event-driven architectures, and enterprise-scale database engineering.

This wasn't junior work: I made architectural decisions, led system design, and kept production running.

At **Deutsche Bundesbank**, I worked on complex, mission-critical SQL systems powering monetary policy applications — validating structured data processes against real enterprise-scale databases that demand precision and reliability. I am not a simple backend developer. I think in systems.
