I wanted a portfolio that is easy to keep current while I focus on engineering work. Markdown is a good fit: it keeps the content portable, reviewable in Git, and independent from a CMS.

Quartz turns those files into a fast website, while preserving useful links between projects, roles, and ideas. For example, [[experience/noyan-fanavar-arya|my backend work]] connects naturally to the systems I am exploring now.

The site design stays classical and quiet. Its warm paper palette and small lapis-and-terra-cotta accent take a subtle cue from Persian architectural colour, without competing with the professional content.
